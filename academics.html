Academic Overview

ABC International School offers a comprehensive and rigorous academic program designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity. Our curriculum is based on the International Baccalaureate (IB) framework, which emphasizes inquiry-based learning, intercultural understanding, and community service. From Pre-Kindergarten to Grade 12, our students engage in a wide range of subjects, including languages, humanities, sciences, mathematics, and arts. With a focus on academic excellence, personal growth, and global citizenship, we prepare our students to succeed in an ever-changing world and make a positive impact in their communities.The school follows an international curriculum designed to foster intellectual curiosity, personal growth, and global citizenship.

Academic Rules

  • Attendance: Regular attendance is mandatory; students with poor attendance may face disciplinary action.
  • Homework: Students must complete and submit homework on time.
  • Examinations: Students must follow examination rules and regulations.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited.

About Faculty

The faculty at ABC International School consists of highly qualified professionals who hold advanced degrees in their respective fields, with many holding Master’s and Doctoral degrees. Our teachers are continuously engaged in professional development, ensuring that they stay current with the latest educational research and best practices. The faculty represents a wide range of disciplines, including science, mathematics, the humanities, arts, languages, and social studies. Each educator is not only skilled in their subject area but also deeply committed to fostering a collaborative and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive.They utilize innovative teaching methods that blend traditional and modern pedagogies, integrating technology to enhance learning experiences. We believe in fostering curiosity,and critical thinking, empowering our students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.
