ABC INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

ABC International School is a premier educational institution that offers a world-class learning experience to students from diverse backgrounds. With a commitment to academic excellence, our school provides a comprehensive curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and character development. Our experienced and dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and supportive community create a nurturing environment that encourages students to reach their full potential and become responsible global citizens.At ABC International School, we believe in giving back to our community. We encourage our students to participate in community service projects, which help to develop their social awareness, empathy, and sense of responsibility. Our students have participated in various community service projects, including charity events, environmental campaigns, and volunteer work.We understand that every student is unique and may require additional support. Our school provides a range of support services, including counseling, academic support, and special needs support. Our dedicated team of teachers and support staff work closely with parents to ensure that every student receives the support they need to succeed.

GENERAL RULES

  • Respect and Discipline: Students are expected to behave respectfully and responsibly towards teachers, staff, and peers.
  • Attendance and Punctuality: Regular attendance and punctuality are essential. Students must arrive on time for classes.
  • Uniform and Grooming: Students must wear the prescribed school uniform and maintain neat and tidy grooming.
  • Safety and Security: Students must follow safety and security protocols, including procedures and emergency contact information.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited.
